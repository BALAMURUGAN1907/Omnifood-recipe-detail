Steps to make

  • 01

    Marinate the chicken

    Mix chicken with yogurt, masala, and spices until fully coated. Cover and let it rest for one hour.

  • 02

    Cook the Rice

    Boil salted water with whole spices and cook basmati rice. Pull it out when it is just 70 percent done.

  • 03

    Crisp the Onions

    Fry thinly sliced onions in hot oil until golden and crispy. Set them aside — they are the soul of biryani.

  • 04

    Build the Base

    Cook the marinated chicken on medium heat until tender and the masala releases its rich oils

  • 05

    Add the Layers

    Spread cooked rice over the chicken in one even layer. Drizzle saffron milk and scatter crispy onions on top.

  • 06

    The Dum

    Seal the pot and cook on low flame for 25 minutes. This slow cooking gives biryani its rich flavor.
